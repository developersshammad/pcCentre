Our Story

Founded in 2010, PC Centre emerged from a vision to bridge the gap between consumers and the rapidly evolving world of computer hardware in Pakistan. What started as a small online platform connecting tech enthusiasts with local dealers has grown into Pakistan's premier destination for high-quality PC components, laptops, monitors, and accessories.

Over the years, we've built a network of authorized dealers across major cities including Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, and Peshawar. Our commitment to authenticity, competitive pricing, and exceptional customer service has made us the trusted choice for gamers, professionals, students, and businesses alike.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To democratize access to premium computer hardware by connecting consumers with verified dealers, ensuring every Pakistani has access to the latest technology at fair prices with comprehensive support.

Our Vision

To become Pakistan's leading technology ecosystem, fostering innovation and empowering communities through reliable tech solutions, expert guidance, and unparalleled customer experiences.
